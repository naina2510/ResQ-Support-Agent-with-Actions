# ShopEase Customer Support Knowledge Base

## About ShopEase
ShopEase is an online marketplace selling electronics, fashion, and home goods
across India. Orders are delivered through our logistics partners BlueDart and
Delhivery. Customer support is available 24x7 through this assistant, and human
agents are available 9 AM to 9 PM IST.

## Shipping and Delivery

### Delivery timelines
Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days for metro cities and 5 to 8
business days for other locations. Express delivery (available at checkout for
Rs. 99) delivers within 1 to 2 business days in metro cities only.

### Order tracking
Every order gets a tracking ID once it is shipped. Customers can track their
order using their Order ID (format: SE followed by 5 digits, for example
SE10023). Tracking updates can take up to 24 hours to reflect after dispatch.

### Delivery delays
If an order has not arrived within the promised window, customers should first
check the live tracking status. If the order shows no movement for more than
48 hours, a support ticket should be raised so the logistics team can
investigate.

## Returns and Refunds

### Return policy
Most items can be returned within 10 days of delivery. Electronics must be
returned within 7 days and must include all original accessories and
packaging. Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned.

### How refunds work
Once a returned item passes quality check (2 to 3 business days after pickup),
the refund is initiated. Refunds to UPI and bank accounts take 3 to 5 business
days. Refunds to credit cards take 5 to 7 business days. Cash-on-delivery
orders are refunded to a bank account provided by the customer.

### Return pickup
Return pickups are free. After a return is approved, pickup is scheduled
within 2 business days. Customers should keep the item packed and ready.

## Payments

### Accepted payment methods
ShopEase accepts UPI, credit and debit cards, net banking, and cash on
delivery (COD). COD is available for orders under Rs. 10,000.

### Payment failed but money deducted
If a payment fails but money was deducted, the amount is automatically
reversed by the bank within 5 to 7 business days. If it is not reversed within
7 business days, the customer should raise a support ticket with the
transaction reference number.

## Cancellations

### Cancelling an order
Orders can be cancelled free of charge any time before they are shipped. Once
an order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled, but the customer can refuse
delivery or return it after delivery as per the return policy.

## Warranty

### Electronics warranty
All electronics come with the manufacturer's standard warranty, typically 12
months. Warranty claims are handled directly by the manufacturer's authorized
service centres. ShopEase can help customers locate the nearest service centre
and provide the invoice copy needed for a claim.

## Account and Security

### Password reset
Customers can reset their password from the login page using the "Forgot
Password" link. A reset link is sent to the registered email and is valid for
30 minutes.

### Suspicious activity
If a customer notices an order they did not place, they should immediately
raise a support ticket and change their password. ShopEase never asks for
passwords or OTPs over chat, phone, or email.
